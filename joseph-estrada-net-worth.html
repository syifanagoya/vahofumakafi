What is Joseph Estrada's Net Worth?

Joseph Estrada is a Filipino politician and actor who has a net worth of $6 million. Joseph Estrada was born in Tondo, Manila, Commonwealth of the Philippines in April 1937. Estrada served as the 13th President of the Philippines from 1998 to 2001. Since 2013 he has served as the Mayor of Manila which is the capital city of the Philippines. He starred in more than 100 films and used his popularity to transition to politics.

FAQs about Ellie Kemper

Who is Ellie Kemper?

She is an American actress and comedian known for her roles as Erin Hannon on The Office and the titular character in Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt. She’s also appeared in movies like Bridesmaids and 21 Jump Street.

What did she study in college?

She attended Princeton University, where she majored in English and was active in the university’s improv group, Quipfire!, and the Princeton Triangle Club.

After college, She moved to New York City and performed with the Upright Citizens Brigade and the People’s Improv Theatre. She gained attention for her comedic roles in various sketches and shows.

Leeland, comprised of Leeland Mooring and Casey Moore, will release their live album, Better Word, on September 13, 2019. Continuing to share their melodically-rich songs and their well-known progressive worship rock influence partnered with Mooring’s calming tenor voice, Better Word is comprised of 12 songs, such as“First Love Fire,” “Inhabit,” “Rain ft. Noel Robinson,” and the title track, “Better Word."

Elizabeth “Beth” Howland (born May 28, 1941) is an American actress who has been on stage and in television productions extensively. Howland is most known for her role as Vera in the comedy Alice, which was based on the iconic Martin Scorsese film Alice Doesn’t Live Here Anymore. Howland was born in New York City and raised in Los Angeles. Also on Broadway, Howland premiered the part of Amy in Stephen Sondheim’s Company, in which she debuted the difficult, fast-paced patter song “Getting Married Today.”
